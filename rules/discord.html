Discord Rules

Be respectful

The wonder Server is a place where you can have fun with your friends, so be respectful to all players. These rules are in place to maintain a welcoming and safe environment for all players.

Use common sense

As long as you think about what you say, no one will get hurt so use common sense.

Privacy & Account security

All users are responsible for their own accounts and personal information. Members also take full responsibility for any actions which may occur on their accounts. DO NOT share any of your account or personal information with anyone!

Listen to staff

Staff is here to keep the discord server orginsized, so listen to them and don't try to argue with them. (If you do think you're wrongfully punished contact contact@thewonderserver.com)

Appropriate channels

Channels each have its purpose, if your conversation is drifting off from the topic of the channel, please move to #general where all appropriate topics are allowed.
