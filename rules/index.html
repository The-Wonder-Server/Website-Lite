Rules

How do we expect you to behave?

The wonder Server is a place where you can have fun with your friends, so be respectful to all players. These rules are in place to maintain a welcoming and safe environment for all players.

How to report a rule-breaking player?

To report a player use the command /report {username}. This will give our staff a notification and they will do their best to resolve your issue.

Privacy & Account security

All players are responsible for their own accounts and personal information. Players also take full responsibility for any actions which may occur on their accounts. DO NOT share any of your account or personal information with anyone!
